Best Free Online Courses & Certifications for Freshers 2026

December 20, 2025 12 min read

Certifications from Google, Microsoft, IBM, and other top companies can significantly boost your resume. The best part? Many of these courses are completely free. Here's our curated list of the best free courses for 2026.

Google Certifications

Google Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate

Learn data analytics skills including SQL, R programming, Tableau, and data visualization. This certificate prepares you for entry-level data analyst roles.

⏱️ 6 months 📚 8 courses 💰 Free audit (paid for certificate) 🎯 Beginner-friendly

Google Google IT Support Professional Certificate

Entry-level IT support skills including troubleshooting, networking, operating systems, and security. No prior experience required.

⏱️ 6 months 📚 5 courses 💰 Free audit 🎯 Best for IT support roles

Google Google UX Design Professional Certificate

Complete UX design training including user research, wireframing, prototyping with Figma, and portfolio building.

⏱️ 6 months 📚 7 courses 💰 Free audit 🎯 High demand field

💡 How to Get Coursera Courses Free

  • Audit option: Click "Enroll for Free" then "Audit the course"
  • Financial Aid: Apply for aid to get certificate free
  • Free trials: 7-day free trial of Coursera Plus
