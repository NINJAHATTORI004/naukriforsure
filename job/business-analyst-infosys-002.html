Business Analyst

Infosys Limited
Hyderabad, Chennai Full-time Posted 3 days ago

Job Description

Join Infosys as a Business Analyst and help bridge the gap between business and technology. Work with global clients to understand their requirements and translate them into technical solutions.

This role offers excellent exposure to various industries and domains, making it perfect for freshers looking to build a career in business analysis.

Key Responsibilities

  • Gather and document business requirements from stakeholders
  • Analyze business processes and identify improvement opportunities
  • Create detailed functional specifications and user stories
  • Collaborate with development teams to ensure requirements are met
  • Conduct UAT (User Acceptance Testing) and validate deliverables
  • Prepare presentations and reports for stakeholders

Requirements

  • B.E/B.Tech/MBA or equivalent degree
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Proficiency in MS Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word)
  • Basic understanding of software development lifecycle
  • Ability to work in a team environment

Skills Required

Business Analysis Excel SQL Requirement Gathering Communication Documentation

Benefits

  • Competitive compensation package
  • Comprehensive health benefits
  • Learning and certification opportunities
  • Global project exposure
  • Fast-track career growth
  • Work from home flexibility
