Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Welcome to NaukriForSure. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address when you sign up for job alerts or create an account
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, clicks on job listings
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to improve your experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our job search services
  • Send job alerts and recommendations based on your preferences
  • Analyze site usage to enhance user experience
  • Communicate important updates about our services
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Analytics Partners: Google Analytics for understanding site usage
  • Advertising Partners: Google AdSense for displaying relevant ads
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences (dark mode, saved jobs)
  • Understand how you use our site
  • Serve relevant advertisements

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Secure data storage practices

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to external job applications and company websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

NaukriForSure is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: naukriforsure@outlook.com
  • Website: Contact Page
