Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my referrals?
Once you share your unique referral link, anyone who signs up using it will be tracked automatically. You can check your referral dashboard to see all your successful referrals, earned rewards, and unlock badges!
When do I receive my rewards?
Rewards are credited once your referred friend completes their profile and applies to at least one job. Badge rewards are instant, while tier benefits are activated within 24-48 hours of reaching the milestone.
Is there a limit to how many friends I can refer?
No limits! Refer as many friends as you want. The more you refer, the more rewards you earn. Top referrers also get featured on our leaderboard and win special monthly prizes.
Can my friends also earn by referring others?
Absolutely! Everyone who joins gets their own unique referral link. Your friends can also start referring and earning rewards. Help build a community of job seekers helping each other!
