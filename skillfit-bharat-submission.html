Overview

SkillFit Bharat AI is a multilingual, mobile-first AI hiring and workforce fitment platform that interviews candidates through video or voice, verifies authenticity, evaluates employability, detects fraud, and matches workers to jobs or training opportunities in minutes.

Key Differentiator: Replaces resume-heavy screening with a skills-first digital assessment system where candidates can simply speak, answer questions, and demonstrate readiness using a smartphone.

Vision

Millions of skilled workers across India—especially blue-collar, semi-skilled, and diploma/ITI candidates—struggle to access formal employment because hiring systems depend on resumes, English fluency, manual interviews, and urban access.

Our Mission: Enable job-ready talent identification at scale through an inclusive digital platform that empowers governments, employers, training institutes, and workforce missions.

Core Problems Being Solved

Candidate Challenges

  • No professional resume
  • Limited English proficiency
  • Strong practical skills but weak formal presentation
  • Rural/district-level location barriers
  • Repeated travel for interviews
  • Fake job scams & unverified hiring
  • No recognized employability profile

Employer/Government Challenges

  • Massive volumes of applicants
  • Slow screening processes
  • Inconsistent interviews
  • Duplicate or fake applications
  • Difficulty hiring multilingual candidates
  • Lack of district-wise skill intelligence
  • No scalable classification of training needs

The Solution

SkillFit Bharat AI provides an intelligent AI interviewer that conducts structured assessments in local languages through multiple modalities:

📹 Video Interview

Full face-to-face assessment with AI

🎙️ Voice Interview

Audio-based assessment for low bandwidth

📱 Mobile Mode

Low-bandwidth optimization for basic devices

🏪 Kiosk Mode

Assisted mode for employment centers

AI Evaluation Metrics
  • Communication clarity and fluency
  • Role-fit knowledge and relevance
  • Confidence indicators and consistency
  • Skill relevance and authenticity signals
  • Overall job readiness level

Innovation: Digital Skill Passport

Every candidate receives a reusable Digital Skill Passport containing:

  • Verified identity confidence score
  • Communication ability score
  • Role readiness score
  • Language capability profile
  • Reliability/consistency score
  • Recommended job families
  • Suggested training modules

This acts as an AI-generated employability profile, especially powerful for users without traditional resumes.
