🏆 Our Verdict: Which Should You Target?

Choose Google if...

You want the highest compensation, best work-life balance, and world-class perks. Ideal if you value innovation and can handle a longer, more rigorous interview process. Best overall choice for most candidates.

Choose Microsoft if...

You want excellent work-life balance with strong job security and a supportive culture. Great for those who value stability and continuous learning. The most "employee-friendly" of the three.

Choose Amazon if...

You're highly ambitious, thrive under pressure, and want rapid career growth. Amazon promotes faster but expects more. Great for building a strong resume, but be prepared for intense work culture.
