Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is NaukriForSure free for job seekers?

Yes! NaukriForSure is completely free for job seekers. You can browse jobs, apply to positions, and access all features at no cost.

How do I apply for a job?

Simply click on any job listing to view the details, then click the "Apply Now" button. You'll be guided through the application process.

Are all jobs verified?

Yes, we verify all job listings before they go live on our platform. This ensures that you only see genuine opportunities from legitimate employers.

Can I get job alerts?

Yes! You can sign up for job alerts and receive notifications when new jobs matching your preferences are posted.
