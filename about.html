About NaukriForSure

Empowering freshers and job seekers to land their dream jobs

Our Story

Why I Built NaukriForSure

I created NaukriForSure because I witnessed a painful reality — talented freshers and job seekers struggling to get placed, not because they lack skills, but because they lack opportunities and exposure.

While students from IITs and NITs have companies lining up at their campus, students from smaller colleges often don't even get a chance to prove themselves. Their college tag becomes a barrier, not their ability.

NaukriForSure was born to change this. This platform aggregates genuine fresher job opportunities from top companies — jobs that don't discriminate based on your college tier.

❧

"Your college doesn't define your potential. Your skills, dedication, and hunger to learn do. NaukriForSure is here to give you that fair chance."

— Ansh Mittal, Founder
